Women’s History Month is a time to recognize the women who are not only advancing their fields, but reshaping what leadership means within them. The theme guiding this year’s Women at 1Password Employee Resource Group (ERG), Leading the Change: Women Shaping a Sustainable Future , reflects that responsibility. One of the women leading that change within 1Password is Nicole Scherbina, Senior Staff Manager of Product Operations and a leader within our Women at 1Password ERG.
