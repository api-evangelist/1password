Modern enterprises aren’t just adding employees; they’re adding subsidiaries, multiple teams, contractors, AI builders, temporary projects, and new SaaS tools every week. And every new addition to a company’s ecosystem also brings more credentials to manage. Unfortunately, not all of those credentials can be managed by solutions like single-sign-on (SSO) or privileged access management (PAM).
