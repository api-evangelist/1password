Enterprise password managers (EPM) like 1Password, LastPass, Dashlane, and Bitwarden make it easy to create, store, and use strong passwords across websites and apps. With features like secure sharing, data encryption, and protection against phishing and malware, these tools help IT and security teams keep credentials safe and enforce company policies. With so many EPM options available, choosing the right one can be difficult.
