Enterprise password managers (EPM) like 1Password, LastPass, Dashlane, and Bitwarden help you create, store, and fill strong passwords and credentials across different websites and apps, so you don’t have to remember or write them down. EPMs provide secure sharing, data encryption, and data breach prevention against phishing and malware, helping IT and Security teams protect and enforce policies around credentials. While there are many EPM options, choosing the best password manager can be a challenge.
