Introducing new features in 1Password Enterprise Password Manager – MSP Edition to reduce client onboarding effort and give MSPs greater control over policies, access, and usage.Setting up and managing client environments often involves repetitive, manual work. Each new managed company requires policy setup, access configuration, and ongoing oversight. Repeating this across environments slows onboarding, introduces inconsistencies, and makes it harder to maintain control.
