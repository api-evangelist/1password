At 1Password, our Jewish 'Bits Employee Community Group exists to create space for Jewish employees and curious allies alike to connect, learn, and show up authentically. For Jewish Heritage Month this May, we wanted to spotlight Nicole Smith , Staff Project Manager and lead of our Jewish 'Bits ECG. In her four years at 1Password, Nicole has been someone people turn to when the work gets complicated, because she builds trust that makes honest conversations possible.
