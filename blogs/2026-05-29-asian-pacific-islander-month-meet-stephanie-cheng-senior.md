May was A&PI Heritage Month, and at 1Password, we're proud to shine a light on the people who bring these perspectives to life in our work and help shape our culture every day. This year, we decided to spotlight Stephanie Cheng, Senior Customer Trainer and a leader within our A&PI Employee Resource Group. With over five years at 1Password, Stephanie has built her career around helping people feel capable, confident, and supported, whether she's onboarding a new customer or creating space for her colleagues to connect and belong.
