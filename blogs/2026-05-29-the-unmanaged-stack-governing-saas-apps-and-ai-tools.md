In the constantly evolving world of enterprise tech, there’s one thing that IT and security teams have always been able to count on: users won’t follow policy if they think it’s standing in the way of their productivity. Case in point: 1Password’s most recent annual report found that 52% of employees have downloaded apps without IT approval.
