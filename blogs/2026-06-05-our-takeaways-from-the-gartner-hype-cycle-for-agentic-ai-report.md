1Password analyzes Gartner's April 2026 Hype Cycle report, which emphasizes that fully autonomous agents are not ready for most enterprise use cases and that human oversight remains essential. The piece explores three agentic use cases - enterprise AI assistants, software engineering agents, and agentic analytics - while recommending three critical governance decisions organizations should make before deploying agents at scale.
