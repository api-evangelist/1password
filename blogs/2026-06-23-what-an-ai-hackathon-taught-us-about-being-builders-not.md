When 1Password announced we were having an AI-themed Hackathon, my first thought was that the content design team had to be involved. Over the last few months, our content design team saw massive changes not only in tech but also in our ways of working. It felt like everything was shifting to AI all at once: how we designed, prototyped, built new features, and collaborated across teams.
