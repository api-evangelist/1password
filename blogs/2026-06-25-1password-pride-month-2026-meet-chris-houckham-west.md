For years, our Pride Employee Resource Group (ERG) has been building something that goes far beyond a single month on the calendar: a community rooted in connection, visibility, and belonging for employees and allies across 1Password. This Pride Month, we're spotlighting Chris Houckham-West, a Customer Success Leader based in EMEA and a leader within our Pride ERG. In his time at 1Password, Chris has built a high-performing Customer Success team, earned recognition as part of President's Club, and helped bring our very first in-person Pride event in EMEA to life.
